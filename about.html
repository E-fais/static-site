Who Are We?

We are a multi service firm offering broad range of services in assurance (audit), advisory (consulting) , compliance, tax and legal domains. We predominantly serve the small and medium sized businesses by taking a holistic approach in addressing client requirements. We are currently present in 5 countries and we are continuing to expand to new territories. With today’s demanding compliance and regulatory landscape along with increasingly challenging market conditions ,we believe organizations require affordable , high quality and timely services from a reliable partner with cross-disciplinary capabilities to help them grow. And that’s where Tass & Hamjit come into picture. Our team includes Chartered Accountants , Certified Management Accountants, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries and Management Experts having experience working with global accounting and consulting firms as well as large corporates.

Why Us?

We break conventional relationship formula between a consultant and client by becoming ourselves a part of the business and through developing trust, confidence and professionalism. We understand our clients root needs and find solutions through various scientific approaches which are communicated to the client in an easily understandable way. Above being assistance we can work along with you to see that you financial management problems are solved in the right way at the right time. We ensure expert knowledge, team work, advanced technologies and precise methodologies at your service.

Growth & beyond

Undergoing continuous improvisations, we are emerging as a one-stop-solution for all accounting and advisory consultancy services. Every service provided by us keeps evolving to catch up with the market; however, what remains the same are our values. Our professional standards are ground in trust, integrity and dedication and guide our relationship building with each client. We uphold these values internally also. By training to equip our employees with skillsets, we bring about their professional development and provide an environment that maintains high employee satisfaction all the while assuring them a clear long-term career path. We believe technology to be the bridge between knowledge and efficiency; hence, we bring about its adaptation internally and also recommend it to our clients as per requirement.
