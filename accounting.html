Accounting

ASAA provides professional accounting services to non-audit clients that wish to outsource their accounting function. Newly setup business and even some established business houses often find it more efficient and cost effective to outsource the accounting function to an external service provider who is not only familiar with international requirements such as IFRS but is also tuned to local requirements. ASAA plays a key role in not only accounting for the day to day transactions but also in preparing the year end financial statements in compliance with accounting standards for presentation to the auditors and the shareholders.
